Forest Modelling

Published: Mon 22 May 2023
Updated: Mon 22 May 2023
By Colm Duffy

In misc.

Forests and afforestation play a pivotal role in mitigating climate change, and the FUSION modelling group recognizes their significance as key land uses in this context. Our expertise in forest modelling allows us to assess the environmental and climate impacts of forest management and afforestation strategies.

Through our bespoke forest models integrated into the GOBLIN (General Overview for a Backcasting approach to Livestock INtensification) land balance model, we provide comprehensive assessments of the potential of forests in carbon sequestration, biodiversity conservation, and sustainable resource management.

Furthermore, our customizations of the tier 3 CBM CFS3 (Carbon Budget Model of the Canadian Forest Sector) forest stock change model with Irish-specific emissions factors enable accurate estimation of carbon stocks and emissions associated with afforestation efforts in Ireland.

By including these advanced forest models in environmental impact assessment scenarios, we support evidence-based decision making and assist policymakers in developing strategies that maximize the climate change mitigation potential of forests and afforestation.

At FUSION, we are committed to advancing sustainable land use practices, and our forest modelling capabilities contribute to the global efforts of mitigating climate change and preserving our planet's invaluable ecosystems.

Our research organization unites scholars from diverse disciplines and adopts a comprehensive "whole system" perspective when it comes to model development.
