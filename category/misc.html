Integrated Assessment Modelling

Published: Mon 22 May 2023
Updated: Mon 22 May 2023
By Colm Duffy

In misc.

Human land use, particularly agriculture, exacts a substantial environmental toll, leading to negative impacts across various categories. Simultaneously, global food systems fail to adequately fulfill the food security requirements of all individuals while squandering significant amounts of resources. Additionally, numerous farmers and other producers do not receive fair compensation for their outputs.

The research outcomes generated by the FUSION modelling group aim to tackle critical policy challenges in the aforementioned domain by providing evidence-based assessments. These assessments aid decision makers and researchers in the development of more sustainable food systems and land use practices.

Our research organization unites scholars from diverse disciplines and adopts a comprehensive "whole system" perspective when it comes to model development.
